- Fixed `trusted-filer-mutate` over-blocking the sanctioned `/resolve-discussion --no-action` path: a `--reason "not planned"` close on a `discussion`-labeled Issue authored by a trusted filer (OWNER/MEMBER/MAINTAINER/COLLABORATOR — the common maintainer-filed case) was short-circuited past discussion classification into the trusted-filer block, forcing a `SKIP_HOOKS` escape on a documented frictionless close. Discussion-tier is now classified first and independent of filer trust: a discussion close allows `--reason completed`/`"not planned"` regardless of filer and blocks bare/`duplicate`; the trusted-filer block applies only to non-discussion Issues.
