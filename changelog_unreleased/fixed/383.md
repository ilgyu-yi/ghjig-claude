- The commit-subject extractor no longer mis-reads a normal `-m` commit as a heredoc message when its body merely mentions a `<<TAG` token: a single `-m` whose first line is a valid Conventional Commit subject is honored as the subject (line-1 precedence), so commits whose body discusses shell/heredocs are no longer wrongly blocked. The genuine `$(cat <<TAG … TAG)` form and fail-safe blocking of malformed subjects are preserved. SPEC §6.1.1. (#383)
