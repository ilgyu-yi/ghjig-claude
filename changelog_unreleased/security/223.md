- Closed a `trusted-filer-mutate` selector-evasion bypass: both sub-arms anchored the issue selector as a bare `[0-9]+`, so `gh issue edit https://…/issues/<N> --remove-label directive` and `gh issue close "<N>"` (URL or quoted-number selectors, both accepted by gh) evaded the matcher entirely — silently declassifying a Directive or auto-closing a trusted-filer Issue. The matcher now recognizes bare-number, quoted-number, and URL selectors, and the close arm normalizes the selector to a pure issue number before the `is_trusted_filer` / discussion-label lookups (which require a bare number) and the block message.
