- New tier-3 substrate workflow `initiative-feedback-label.yml` projects `/initiative-feedback`'s comment markers into labels for the orchestrator: an `issue_comment` whose body begins with `## Initiative challenge` gets `initiative:challenged`, and one beginning with `## Initiative completion` gets `initiative:completion-requested` (an unrelated comment gets neither). The two labels are added to `scripts/ensure_v3_labels.sh` and the workflow installs via the existing tier-3 glob. The label is applied by the target-repo CI Action, never by eng's runtime, preserving the `initiative-readonly` boundary. (#359)
