- SPEC §9.2 (issue template spec) gains an issue-**title** principle: the title states the need as explicitly and simply as possible (a plain problem statement, the primary recall/triage index into the issue as durable-memory), and is **not** a `<type>(#N):` commit/PR subject (§10.2 / §10.3) — that form conflates the change with the problem and a leading `#N` reads as a spurious parent-directive indicator. Stated as a guiding norm, not a rigid format or a hard lint/gate; `.claude/templates/issue.md` carries a one-line thin-pointer hint. (#583)
